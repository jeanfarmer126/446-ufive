Frequently Asked Questions ?

What is the purpose of the website?
March Madness is a month of tension and stress. We are here to assist in the picking of your bracket and learning what it is that is necessary to succeed.
While the probability is low that you'll get it exactly right, we'll help you do better than anybody you're competing against.
How do you choose an upset?
Analyze the team's current streak and compare strength of schedule's between close seeds.
Where should I post a bracket?
You can post your bracket and enter competitions for free on any majors sporting website.
Which rounds matter the most?
Picking strong teams looking for deep runs is much more important than hitting a first round upset that goes out in the second.
How likely am I to get every pick right?
1 in 9,223,372,036,854,775,808. Stick to the lottery if you want a better chance at 100 percent.
